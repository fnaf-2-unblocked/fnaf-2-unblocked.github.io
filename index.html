Controls for Five Nights at Freddy's 2

Basic Controls

  • Mouse - Used to interact with most elements on the screen, including the cameras, lights, and mask.
  • Mouse Cursor - Move the cursor to interact with buttons and objects.

Office Controls

  • Move Mouse Left/Right - Look to the left or right of the office.
  • Ctrl (Control) Key (when looking left or right): Use the flashlight to illuminate the hallways.
  • Space Bar - Use the flashlight in the main hallway.

Mask Controls

  • Ctrl (Control) Key (while looking forward): Use the Freddy Fazbear Head mask to hide from animatronics.
  • Mouse Click - Click the mask icon at the bottom of the screen to quickly put on or take off the mask.

Camera System

  • Mouse Click - Click on the monitor to open the camera system.
  • Mouse Cursor - Move between camera feeds by clicking on the desired room/camera.
  • Click the Left/Right Arrows - To switch between different camera feeds.

Music Box

  • Camera Feed (CAM 11) - Click and hold the “Wind Up Music Box” button to wind up the music box. This is crucial for preventing the Puppet from attacking.

Ventilation System

  • Check Vent Cameras - To monitor the air vents on the sides of the office for animatronics.

Tips

  • Monitor Power - There is no power limit like in the first game, but you need to manage the flashlight battery.
  • Mask Quickly - Be fast when putting on the Freddy Fazbear Head if an animatronic is in the office.
